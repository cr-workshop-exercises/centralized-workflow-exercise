# Chewy chocolate chip cookies

# for 12 cookies

# Ingredientss

- 100 g granulated sugar
- 165 g brown sugar
- 1 tsp salt
- 115 g butter, melted
- 10 egg
- 1 tsp vanilla ekstrakt
- 155 g flour
- 1/2 tsp baking soda
- 110 g milk chocolate chunks
- 110 g dark chocolate chunks

# Preparation
Whisk sugar, salt, butter until you have a paste with no lumps.
Whisk egg and vanilla.
Sift flour and baking soda, fold under.
Fold in the chocolate chunks, then chill the dough (and yourself) for at least 30 minutes.
For a more intense toffee-like flavor and deeper color, chill the dough overnight.
Preheat oven to 180°C. Scoop dough onto a baking tray, 10cm between cookies!
Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the edges have started to barely brown.
